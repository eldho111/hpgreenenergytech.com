Our Mission & Vision

Build affordable, efficient green-hydrogen and ammonia-fuel solutions for India — replacing diesel and reducing grid volatility with safe, liquid-fuel logistics.

  • Mission: Deliver bankable H₂ assets for mobility, industry, and microgrids.
  • Vision: Net-zero energy access at scale using renewables + NH₃/H₂.
  • Status: Prototyping stacks & integration; pilots with partners.
